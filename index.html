ORGANIC APPLES

Organic apples are often fresher, as they are not treated with chemicals to extend their shelf life. They may taste better, as they are often allowed to ripen fully on the tree rather than being picked before they are fully ripe. Organic apples may be more nutritious, as they are grown in soil that has been allowed to rest and regenerate.
